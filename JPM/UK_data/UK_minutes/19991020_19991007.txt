MINUTES OF
MONETARY POLICY
COMMITTEE MEETING
6 and 7 October 1999
These are the minutes of the Monetary Policy Committee meeting held on
6 and 7 October 1999.
They are also available on the Internet (http://www.bankofengland.co.uk/mpc9910.pdf).
The Bank of England Act 1998 gives the Bank of England operational responsibility
for setting interest rates to meet the Government(cid:213)s inflation target.  Operational
decisions are taken by the Bank(cid:213)s Monetary Policy Committee.  The Committee meets
on a regular monthly basis and minutes of its meetings are released on the
Wednesday of the second week after the meeting takes place.  Accordingly, the
minutes of the Committee meeting held on 3 and 4 November will be published on
17 November 1999.MINUTES OF THE MONETARY POLICY COMMITTEE MEETING
ON 6-7 OCTOBER 1999
