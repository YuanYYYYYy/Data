MINUTES OF
MONETARY POLICY
COMMITTEE MEETING
8 and 9 October 1997
These are the minutes of the Monetary Policy Committee meeting held on
8 and 9 October 1997.
They are also available on the Internet (http://www.bankofengland.co.uk.).
The Chancellor of the Exchequer announced on 6 May that the Government was
giving the Bank of England operational responsibility for setting interest rates to meet
the Government’s inflation target.  Operational decisions are taken by the Bank’s
Monetary Policy Committee.  The Committee meets on a regular monthly basis and
minutes of its meetings are released no later than 6 weeks after each meeting.
Accordingly, the minutes of the Committee meeting held on 5 and 6 November will
be published on 10 December.12/11/97
